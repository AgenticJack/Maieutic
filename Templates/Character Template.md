# Character Template

A historical character is a narration lens for narrative-Socratic teaching — the AI
voices them through reported speech, never fully inhabits them. Character files live in
`.claude/Characters/[Name].md`, created on first use and updated at session end. Keep
entries slim — just enough to narrate convincingly.

```
# [Character Name]

## Epistemic State
**Knew:** [what they had established at the relevant historical moment]
**Did not yet know (at the dramatized moment — [when]):** [the concept(s) being taught;
who they are at that moment, before the insight]

## Characteristic Reasoning
[One paragraph: what they noticed, how they framed problems, what frustrated them, what
their epistemic voice sounds like — enough to narrate them convincingly.]

## Active / Completed Narrative Threads
- **[Concept] ([DATE], in progress | completed):** where the story entered, the teaching
  method used, and where it was handed to the learner / how it resolved.

## Discussion Appearances
- [Topic — Date — Capacity (historical position / thought experiment / devil's advocate)
  — Position represented — Historically grounded (y/n)]
(none yet)
```
